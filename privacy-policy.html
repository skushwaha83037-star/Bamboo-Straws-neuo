​Privacy Policy

1. What Info We Grab

When you use our stuff, we might grab:

a. Account Stuff
Your name, email, phone (if you want), and company name

Your login info (passwords are scrambled)

b. Subscription &amp; Payment Stuff
How you pay (our payment buddies handle it securely)

Your payment history and what you like

c. How You Use Stuff
What you do in the app (like clicks and what you use)

When you log in, your IP address, and what device/browser you're on

Reports if something breaks

d. What You Put In
Stuff you upload or make on our thing (like projects, docs, your own stuff)

2. How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

Let you into our platform and use its stuff

Handle your subscription and tell you about payments

Make things personal and work better for you

Answer your questions or help you out

Keep our platform safe and working well

Tell you when we change stuff or have important alerts

You can stop getting our marketing emails whenever.

3. Who Sees Your Info

We don't sell your personal info.

But we might share it with:

Folks we trust, like cloud companies, analytics people, email helpers, or payment processors

The law, if they tell us to or have a good reason

Someone who buys us or takes over (we'll tell you if that happens)

Everyone we work with has to keep your info safe.

4. How Long We Keep Your Info

We keep your info as long as you have an account or need it for legal stuff. If you close your account, we might keep it safe or delete it if you ask.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to:

Keep you logged in

Remember what you like

See how you use our platform

Make things better

You can control cookies in your browser.

6. Is It Safe?

We use normal safety stuff:

HTTPS encryption

Checking for problems all the time

Keeping data separate and scrambled

Only letting the right people in

But, no system is perfect, so use a good password and turn on two-factor if you can.

7. What You Can Do

You might be able to:

See what info we have on you

Fix wrong info

Delete or move your info

Say no to some things we do with your info

Take back your permission whenever

8. Kids

Our stuff is not for kids under 13. We don't try to get info from them.

9. Changes

We might change this policy. If we do, we'll change the date up top and tell you by email or in the app.
